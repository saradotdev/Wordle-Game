How To Play

● Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.

● If you guess a letter that is in the right place, it is shown as GREEN.

● If you guess a letter that is in the word but not in the right place, it is shown as YELLOW.

● If you guess a letter that is not in the word in any place, it is shown as GREY.

● Guess the Wordle in 6 tries to WIN the game!
